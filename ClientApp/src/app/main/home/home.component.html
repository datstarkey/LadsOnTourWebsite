Lads on Tour is a smart-casual guild that was founded back at the beginning of Legion, with most of the core player base having played together since Cataclysm. We are a core team of friends and raiders who have decided to make a comeback for Shadowlands with a bit more motivation than previously.

The aim is to clear the content as fast as we can in 2 days whilst still having fun in the process. We take a big attitude towards playing effectively, this involves doing things outside of raid to focus on just raiding during raid times. We are also aware that mistakes do happen and not every person can perform 100% at all times, all we ask is that you don't make the same mistake twice.
